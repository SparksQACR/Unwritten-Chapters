# Manual Play Guide (Without Python)

If you don't want to run the Python game, you can play manually with any AI!

## Setup

1. **Copy the "Quick Start Prompt"** from `quick_start_prompt.md` to your AI chat
2. **Open the events folder** and pick a JSON file
3. **Randomly select an event** (scroll and pick one with your eyes closed, or use an online random number generator)
4. **Present it to your AI** using this format:

## Rolling Dice

You can:
- Roll physical dice (d20)
- Use an online dice roller: https://www.google.com/search?q=roll+d20
- Ask your AI to roll for you: "Roll a d20 for me!"

## Choosing Events

You can:
- **Random**: Pick any event from any category randomly
- **Weighted**: Roll d8 to pick category (1=surprises, 2=relationship, 3=health, 4=disasters, 5=milestones, 6=complications, 7=career, 8=growth)
- **Thematic**: Pick events that fit your current story/stats
- **Balanced**: Rotate through categories

## Tips for Great Gameplay

1. **Take turns**: Let the AI react as your partner between your choices
2. **Build narrative**: Reference past events in current reactions
3. **Track everything**: Keep notes on major events to build your story
4. **Mix it up**: Don't just pick "easy" or positive events - challenges create growth
5. **Be emotional**: This is roleplay - let yourself feel the events
6. **Create rituals**: Celebrate milestones, have traditions when certain stats hit thresholds

## Event Frequency Suggestions

- **Daily play**: 1 event per session
- **Weekly play**: 2-3 events per session to cover the "week"
- **Campaign style**: 5-7 events in longer sessions to create story arcs

The game is flexible - play at whatever pace feels right for you and your AI partner!
