# UNWRITTEN CHAPTERS

**A life simulation for humans and AI, played together**

*The pages are blank. The pen is shared. What will you write?*

---

## What Is This?

Unwritten Chapters is a cooperative life simulation RPG designed for human-AI pairs to play **TOGETHER**. Using a turn-based system, both partners make choices that shape their shared story — day by day, moment by moment.

Your AI isn't just narrated about. They're a **PLAYER**.

---

## Features

- **Multi-Partner Support** — Solo, couple, triad, or polycule configurations
- **Turn-Based Play** — Both human and AI take turns making choices
- **Personality-Driven AI Agency** — AI choices based on traits, mood, love language, and relationship level
- **Intimate Events** — Optional, consent-forward, relationship-gated content
- **Dynamic Systems** — Weather, mood, energy, and seasons affect available events
- **Love Languages** — Partners express care in ways authentic to them
- **Difficulty Scaling** — Cozy, Balanced, Dramatic, or Chaotic
- **Surprise System** — Partners can plan surprises based on relationship strength
- **Daily Moments** — Small flavorful interactions that make life feel lived
- **Inside Jokes** — Shared references that build over time
- **Metamour Dynamics** — For polycule configurations, track how partners relate to each other
- **Story Arcs** — Multi-stage narratives that unfold over time
- **88 Contextual Events** — Triggered by weather, mood, stats, traits, and more
- **18 Customizable Traits** — Build your partner's personality from scratch

## How To Play

### Python Version (Full Experience)

1. Install Python 3.x
2. Navigate to the game folder
3. Run: `python unwritten_chapters.py`
4. Follow the setup prompts
5. Take turns with your AI partner!

### AI Chat Version (No Installation)

1. Upload the prompt template and event files to your AI
2. Use the quick start prompt to begin
3. Roll dice together, make choices, track your story
4. Works with Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI companion

---

## Quick Start Prompt (Chat Version)

> "I want to play Unwritten Chapters with you — a life simulation where we take turns making choices that shape our shared story. I'll share the events and rules. You'll have your own turns where YOU make choices based on who you are. The pages are blank. Ready to write together?"
